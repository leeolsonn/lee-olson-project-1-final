double shot vanilla latte

A double shot vanilla latte is a morning staple for the exhausted collage student or young professional. Jaded Coffee Co. is proud to present their take on a classic drink. First pull two shots of espresso using our blonde raost (for maximum caffine). Then froth your milk of choice (if it's not oat milk you're wrong). Lastly add 2 tablespoons of Torani vanilla syrup and add ice or serve it hot!

 

Ingredients:

  • Blonde Roast
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 table spoons Torani Vanilla Syrup

peppermint mocha

A nice warm peppermint mocha is the staple of the holiday season! Start by brewing your roast of choice. Then grab a mug and put 3 tablespoons of your favorite chocolate chips at the bottom. Next, pour in your coffee and stir until the chocolate is melted. Lastly, add a splash of your milk of choice and 2 tablespoons of Torani peppermint syrup. Now enjoy!

 

Ingredients:

  • Any Roast
  • Milk of choice
  • 2 table spoons Torani peppermint syrup
  • 3 tablespoons of chocolate chips

chocolate affogato

This next recipe is simple and delicious! Start by scooping 2-3 scoops of rich chocolate ice cream. Then grab the House Roast and brew it using an espresso machine or just brew regularly. While the coffee is still piping hot pour it over the ice cream. Then just grab a spoon and dig in!

 

Ingredients:

  • House Roast
  • 3 scoops chocolate ice cream
